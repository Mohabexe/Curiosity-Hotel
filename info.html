Rooms and Suites

Standard Room

  • Spacious room with modern furnishings and amenities.
  • Comfortable king or queen-sized bed.
  • Private bathroom with a shower or bathtub.
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi and flat-screen TV.

  • Luxurious suite featuring a separate living area and bedroom.
